History

Founded in the year 1984, WWR Rafting Adventures began with a small group of passionate river enthusiasts. Over the decades, we've expanded our reach to offer incredible rafting experiences across the globe. From humble beginnings, we've grown into one of the most recognized names in the rafting industry. Our legacy is one of adventure, friendship, and, of course, unforgettable white-water thrills.
